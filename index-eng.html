EBEC Challenge Coimbra

2nd, 3rd, & 4th of March

EBEC (European BEST Engineering Competition) Challenge is a 24H engineering competition that is totally free and open to all FCTUC students in which they are challenged to solve real life problems assigned by companies and to develop their soft/hard skills.It has two distinct categories: Case Study and Team Design, a theoretical and a practical format, respectively.

During the competition the students will be in direct contact with company members and university professors that will support and aid the teams in order to help with their problems. In the end, the participants will present their projects to a jury constituted by Engineers, Professors and Students, that will pick the winner in each category.

The winners will advance to the final fase - EBEC Challenge Portugal

Case Study

The category of Case Study consists in solving two theoretical problems assigned by a company, the students have to find achievable and efficient solutions that are applicable on a daily basis to solve those problems.

Team Design

The category of Team Design consists in the creation of a prototype that solves a problem assigned by a company. It´s a practical problem and the students have a limited amount of materials that they can use.

The competition is open to all students that are enrolled in FCTUC.
