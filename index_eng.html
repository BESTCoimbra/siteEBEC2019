BEST Coimbra

The Local BEST Group of Universidade de Coimbra, with over 90 members, has the goal to develop the science and technology students of the university through engineering competitions, job fairs and workshops.

EBEC Programe

EBEC Coimbra is one of over 80 local rounds of the biggest Engineering Competition in Europe.

In Coimbra, it’s characterised by being a 24h competition open to all the students of Faculdade de Ciências e Tecnologia of the Universidade de Coimbra, completely free, that tasks its participants to solve real-world problems proposed by companies, all while developing their hard and soft skills.

It is made of two categories: Case Study and Team Design.

Case Study

The Case Study challenge is a test to the teams’ research abilities and originality in a short amount of time.

It’s an excellent scouting opportunity in terms of the participants skills, and a great way for you to obtain multiple and creative solutions in just 24 hours.

Team Design

The category of Team Design challenges the teams in terms of work methods, resilience, teamwork and practical skills.

At the end of the competition, you will be presented with a wide range of prototypes and solutions to the proposed problem.
