BEST Coimbra

The Local BEST Group of Universidade de Coimbra, with 76 members, has the goal to develop the science and technology students of the university throughout all kind of engineering competitions, job fairs and workshops

EBEC Project

EBEC Coimbra is one of over 50 local rounds of the biggest Engineering Competition in Europe.

In Coimbra, it’s characterised by being a 24h competition open to all students of Faculty of Sciences of Technology of the University of Coimbra, completely free, that tasks its participants to solve real-world problems proposed by companies, all while developing their hard and soft skills.

It is made of two categories: Case Study and Team Design.

Case Study

The Case Study challenge is a test to the teams’ research abilities and originality in a short amount of time. It’s an excellent scouting opportunity in terms of the participants skills, and a great way for you to obtain multiple and creative solutions in just 24 hours.

Team Design

The category of Team Design challenges the teams in terms of work methods, resilience, teamwork and practical skills.

At the end of the competition, you will be presented with a wide range of prototypes and solutions to the proposed problem.
